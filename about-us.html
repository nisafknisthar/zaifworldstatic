Water Proofing

In Construction, a Building or Structure needs water proofing since concrete itself will not be water tight. Zaif Tiles team of water proofing has better solution against Leakages, Seepages and Dampness.

Water is perhaps the best friend and the worst foe of concrete. Concrete needs a calculated amount of water to facilitate strength development by the hydration of cement and to attain desired workability; at the same time , any excess water results in the formation of voids of varying sizes in the concrete matrix, resulting in serious durability problems later on in the services life of the structure. Thus it is very essential that the amount of water to be added into concrete is gauged correctly and the use of water reducing admixture like plasticizers and super plasticizers be advocated. A dense, impermeable concrete is the key to durability and this is the first line of defense against deteriorating agents.

Ensuring Dense Concrete :

Proper control of quality in construction and using good materials is perhaps the best way to ensure dense, durable and reliable concrete. The water-cement ratio is to be kept as low as possible so that the formation of voids by trapped excess water is minimized. To aid in reducing water-cement ratio and at the same time ensuring workability admixture like plasticizers and super plasticizers and integral water proofing compounds in the form of powder or liquid are recommended.

Weather Proofing

We have the simplest technical remedy to stop the leakage of R.C.C. Roof or any other flat Roof including Stone Slab Roof. It is very Economical, Eco-friendly and easy to lay. This is the most effective insulator against extreme climate conditions. Due to the heat in the day time the slab will expand and cooling in the night will lead to contraction causing minute cracks initially on the ordinary cement Roof slab. Once the crack widens the water will enter into R.C.C. slab and start wetting the inner surface which will lead to the falling of water in droplets. The iron bars will start corroding causing increase in volume, consequently enlargement of the crack will cause more leakage of Roof. If not checked in time, the strength of the Building will be deteriorated considerably. Split Tiles are an excellent remedy to prevent the leakage.

SPLIT TILES

Split tiles are manufactured using the most latest technology in this field. After the selection of various types of clay they are proportionately mixed using Mixer, Crusher and fine grinder before extrusion through polisher. Clay is kept for ageing before final extrusion through De-airing Machine. De-Airing is the latest method in Terracotta wherein entire air from the clay molecules is drawn out using high power vacuum pumps. This process will render more compatibility, strength and fine finish to the Tiles.
